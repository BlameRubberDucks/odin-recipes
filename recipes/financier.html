Oui Oui, Financier!

Description

Financiers are small and buttery almond cakes commonly found in French patisserie. The cakes are absolutely delicious, with sponge cake texture, buttery and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 30g almond flour
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 80g powdered sugar
  • 50g butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • sliced almonds (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine almond flour and all-purpose flour together. Using a stand mixer or electronic hand mixer.
  2. Beat the egg white and powdered sugar until snowy or until soft peaks form.
  3. Gently incorporate the flour mixture. Slowly add butter and vanilla extract until the batter becomes smooth. DO NOT over mix.
  4. Transfer the batter into a piping bag or a plastic bag, with one of the edges snipped off. Add the batter into financiers mold. Top each financier cake with a few sliced almonds. Bake at 220°C (428°F) for 12 minutes.
  5. Let cool for 15 minutes before removing the financiers from the mold. Enjoy!
